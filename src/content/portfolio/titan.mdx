## We developed the Titan wall mount app to make it easy for consumers around the world to find compatible wall mount products for their TVs and displays.

This Wall Mounts App makes selecting the correct wall mount for your plasma or led TV easy. Enter your TV make, model and size and select from the available mounts. With instructional videos and a tool checklist, the app also makes installation simple too.
